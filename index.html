Pravasi Odia Vikas Samiti (POVS) is a not for proﬁt socio-cultural organization. It was created by some nationalist non-resident Odias who regularly met and discussed about the state of non-resident Odias and resident Odias and how they will live a comfortable and digniﬁed life and their contribution to the Development of Odisha and the Nation. In the year of 2012 it was shaped and registered in 2014 in New Delhi vide registration number district east/society/1451/2015. POVS connects the Odia diaspora staying beyond the boundary of Odisha to be tuned with the root and assimilate with the heritage, tradition, culture, literature, history and present day development. It mobilises the intent of the people for the development of the Odia diaspora in and beyond Odisha.
